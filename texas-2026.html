Water

Cool off

Late August runs hot. A spring-fed swim, a rooftop deck, or a full float down the Guadalupe.

Tube the GuadalupeNew Braunfels · ~45 min
Map
Rent through Rockin' R, rope the tubes together, float a cooler between you. Cold dam-fed water. If the river's low, switch to the spring-fed Comal. Eat at the Gristmill in Gruene before or after.
Barton Springs PoolAustin
Map
Three acres of 70° spring water with a skyline view. A few dollars to get in.

Map

Roads

Where to drive

The Bronco is the classroom. Big highway miles between cities, twisty Hill Country once you're settled.

Dallas to Austin, the back wayUS-281
Map
Skip the interstate. Two-lane miles through Glen Rose (dinosaur tracks and the Fossil Rim drive-through safari), Hico, Lampasas, and Burnet. Calmer practice, better scenery.
Around AustinHill Country
Map
RR 2222 and RR 620 around Lake Travis, Hamilton Pool Road to Dripping Springs, and the climb up Mount Bonnell.
The Twisted SistersSkip this trip
Map
RR 335/336/337 west of the Hill Country — legendary, but tight switchbacks two-plus hours out. Save it for a future run.
