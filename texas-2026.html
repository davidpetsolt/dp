The license

At 18 Cameron goes straight for a full license, no permit step. Two courses must be done first.

Book the DPS appointment early

Appointment only at txdpsscheduler.com — slots go fast. Garland Mega Center often has the best availability; a closer North Dallas option may exist (verify).

Garland Mega Center mapBook a slot

Before the test

  • 6-hour Adult Driver Ed

    Online, required for ages 18–24.

  • ITAD video

    1-hour online, required for first-time applicants. Certificate is valid only 90 days, so do it early-to-mid August.

  • Print both certificates

    Digital copies are not accepted at the test.

  • Bring

    Passport, Social Security card, and two proofs of Texas residency.
